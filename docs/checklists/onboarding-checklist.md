# Onboarding Checklist

**Owner:** Onboarding Mentor  
**Related roles:** [Roles & Personas](../octoacme-roles-and-personas.md)

## Purpose
Ensure new team members have the access, context, and support they need to become productive contributors as quickly as possible.

## Before Day 1 (Onboarding Mentor & HR)

- [ ] Accounts created (email, Slack, GitHub, project management tool)
- [ ] Hardware and development environment provisioned
- [ ] New team member added to relevant teams and repos
- [ ] Welcome message sent with first-day instructions
- [ ] Calendar invites for first-week meetings sent

## Day 1 — Orientation

- [ ] Introductions to team members completed
- [ ] Company / team communication channels explained
- [ ] Core process docs shared: [Project Management Overview](../octoacme-project-management-overview.md), [Roles & Personas](../octoacme-roles-and-personas.md)
- [ ] Development environment setup walkthrough completed
- [ ] First-week schedule reviewed with new team member

## Week 1 — Getting Up to Speed

- [ ] Codebase / repository walkthrough completed
- [ ] Key architectural decisions and design docs reviewed
- [ ] Team conventions and coding standards covered
- [ ] Access verified: repo, CI/CD, staging environment, dashboards
- [ ] First small task or "good first issue" assigned
- [ ] Daily check-in with Onboarding Mentor scheduled

## Week 2–4 — Integration

- [ ] First contribution (PR or equivalent) submitted and reviewed
- [ ] Team ceremonies attended (standups, planning, retrospective)
- [ ] Questions log reviewed and open items resolved
- [ ] Introductions to cross-functional partners (QA, PM, PdM) completed
- [ ] Role-specific responsibilities and goals clarified with team lead

## Onboarding Complete

- [ ] New team member confirms they feel ready to work independently
- [ ] Onboarding Mentor documents any gaps or improvements for future onboarding
- [ ] Onboarding Mentor role transitions to general team support
